ABOUT H&I CONSTRUCTION

INDUSTRIAL CONSTRUCTION EXPERTS

Founded in 2016, H&I Construction LLC brings industrial-grade solutions to commercial and municipal projects across southeastern Washington. Our team combines heavy equipment expertise with precision craftsmanship to deliver projects on spec and on time.
